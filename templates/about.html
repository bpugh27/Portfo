03 : About me

Introduction

Hello, I'm Brandon Pugh, a student studying computer science at Jacksonville State University. I am passionate about creating new innovations that will help today's society become more efficient. Throughout my computer science journey, I have cultivated a unique blend of skills and qualities that set me apart. I am known for my hardworking attitude, persistence, communication, and creativity.

On this journey I have faced many hardships such as programs not running correctly and feeling left out because I was completly new to this field. I played football at Jacksonville State University and never saw anything else beyond football at one point of time, I suffered an injury and sadly put the game behind me. That injury was the best thing that could have happen to me honestly. It helped me to realize that there is life outside of football. Football helped me to learn valuable traits and being discipline and hardworking happens to be the two that I soaked in the most.

I sucked at coding until my senior year if I am being completly honest. I had to sit down and question myself and figure out what was holding me back from being able to code without any help. I learned that I was the problem the whole time. I had to change my approach to how I was viewing coding and the assests that computer science would bring. I applied the traits that I learned from football and I must say I see transformation as I continue to grow and flourish in coding.

I have taken courses outside of my univeristy in order to expand my growth with coding and I have loved every minute of it.
